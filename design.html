A lot of my research work involves the design of new parts. In particular I like integrating new materials into my research work that allow access to new experimental conditions e.g. superalloys or novel ceramics. I am also interested in designing parts that improve exisiting methods e.g. internal load cell, or probe physical properties of rock e.g. acoustic velocity. I am familiar with Inventor and have experience designing high pressure components.
